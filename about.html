More about me...

I'm a brand & UX/UI Designer based in Brisbane & Gold Coast focused on designing and building user-friendly, impactful designs. I studied Applied Arts, and my skills have been shaped on the job through collaboration with peers, hands-on delivery, and personal study.

In your team you'll find me confident contributor, leading teams through complex design processes. I've guided projects from concept to completion across diverse domains and user groups. I've worked across sectors including Dentsu and Syneos Health, where I've been involved from early ideas through to delivery.

My approach combines strong UX methodology with a background in graphic design and marketing, along with technical skills, making me both a specialist and a versatile collaborator.
